Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how LinkLite ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency regarding how your data is handled.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

LinkLite does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII). All usage data, including links created or managed through our tool, remains strictly within your device's browser and local storage. No data is transmitted to or stored on our servers.

2. Local Storage

LinkLite uses your browser's local storage to save your data for convenience. This data is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties. You are responsible for managing and clearing your browser data as needed.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies, web beacons, or any tracking technologies. Your browsing activities on LinkLite are not monitored, analyzed, or shared.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Since we do not collect any personal or behavioral data, we do not and cannot share your information with third parties. We have no partnerships or agreements with advertisers or analytics providers.

5. Data Security

Although LinkLite does not store your data, we recommend that users take standard precautions to protect their own devices and local data, such as using antivirus software, enabling secure browser settings, and avoiding public devices.

6. Legal Basis for Data Handling

LinkLite operates under a privacy-by-design philosophy. As we do not process user data, we are in compliance with applicable data protection regulations, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. As we do not collect any data, no information is knowingly collected from minors. Parents or guardians are encouraged to supervise their children's online activities.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and the date of the last revision will be noted below. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us via the method provided on our official website.
